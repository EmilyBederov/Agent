B's Breakfast Bagel

Ingredients

  • bagels: 2
  • cream cheese: 4
  • avocado: 1
  • ham: 6

Instructions

Toast the bagels. Meanwhile, cut and pit avocado. Once bagels are toasted, spread with half the bagel with 2 tablespoons cream cheese. Take one half of the avocado, spoon it out, and spread it on other half of bagel. Add 3 slices of ham. Enjoy!
